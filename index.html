Welcome to the Durga Puja Guide

Durga Puja is the biggest festival celebrated with grandeur and enthusiasm in West Bengal. Explore the rich traditions, visit iconic pandals, and immerse yourself in the festive spirit. Whether you're a local or a visitor, this guide will help you navigate through the best pandals and plan your itinerary with ease.
